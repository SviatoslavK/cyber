Strategy

EASY TO PLAY

HARD TO WIN

  • Energy collection
  • Hit
  • Special stones
  • Special stones
  • Luck and Chaos
  • Energy collection

    The player gets energy by collecting 3 or more stones in a row of red, white, yellow and blue colors which are corresponding to energies.Energy are given for each collected:
    3 stones = 1 energy
    4 stones = 1 energy and laser
    5 stones = 1 energy and diamond
    5 stones in angle wise = 1 energy and bomb

  • Hit

    As soon as player collected 4 energy of one element, he attacks the opponent.He chooses the energy, by which he will break the defence of the opponent.
    Variants of attack:
    Player breaks the defense of the opponent. The number of lives deducted equal to the number of the attacking energy.
    The opponent guessed the hitter energy. Life does not deduct.
    Energy, which the player is attacking, always zero out

  • Special stones

    Once the enemy is attacking, the player chooses energy for defense.
    Variants of protection:
    The player guessing attacking energy. Life won’t be removed.
    The player did not guessing attacking energy. Removed as many lives as were of the attacking energy, the protection energy is zero out.
    A player pulled the shield (pass). Energy do not zero out. Removed as many lives as were the attacking energy

  • Special stones

    Laser corresponds to the color of the collected energy (4 stones). By matching laser with two or more stones of the same color, the laser explodes all stones in a row.Clicking on the bomb explodes all nearby standing 12 stones in lozenge. If there are more than one bomb on the play area, they are expired with a chain reaction and explodes more stones.Brilliant (internal currency of the game). In the place of collecting 5 stones of the same color the "diamond" is formed. When the diamond matching with a stone he explodes all gems of that color from the play board

  • Luck and Chaos

    Stones of Success (green) and Stones of Chaos (purple)Collecting of Success Stones by itself don’t give any privileges, it affects on the result by collection of Chaos Stones.
    Variants:
    Chaos give you an energy or take it away (50/50). Collected one chaos and one red, randomly took away red energy (not charged)
    When collecting the energy and chaos you increases the chance of getting more bombs
